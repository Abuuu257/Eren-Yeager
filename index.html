Introduction


Attack on Titan is a gripping story set in a world where humanity lives in fear. Encased within towering walls, they seek refuge from colossal, man-eating humanoids known as Titans. For centuries, this has been their reality, a fragile existence on the brink of annihilation.

The tale follows Eren Yeager, a young boy whose life is irrevocably changed when the seemingly impregnable walls are breached, unleashing a horde of Titans upon his hometown. Witnessing the tragic loss of his mother, Eren vows to exterminate every Titan, fueling a relentless pursuit of vengeance.

Join Eren, childhood crush Mikasa Ackerman, and their childhood friend Armin Arlert as they embark on a perilous journey to uncover the truth behind the Titans and the world beyond the walls. Packed with intense action, thought-provoking themes, and shocking revelations, Attack on Titan is a masterpiece that will keep you on the edge of your seats.


Characters

Eren Yeager

He is a young man who has the unique ability to transform into a Titan, a humanoid creature with superhuman strength. Eren joins the Scout Regiment, an elite group of soldiers
fighting against the Titans, to exact revenge against the Titans that destroyed his mother and many others. He is known for his strong sense of justice and determination to protect
his friends and the world.



Zeke Yeager

He is the captain of the Scout Regiment and is known for his exceptional combat skills, leadership abilities, and determination to reclaim the world for humanity. Zeke's past and motivations are shrouded in mystery, adding to his enigmatic character. His storylines and relationships with other characters have garnered significant attention from fans of the series.

Levi Ackerman

He is a main protagonist of the series and a strong fighter of the Scout Regiment, known for his exceptional combat skills and unwavering dedication to humanity's fight against the Titans. As the deputy captain of the Scout Regiment, Levi is highly respected by his comrades for his bravery, strategic thinking, and unrelenting pursuit of Titans. His character has gained widespread popularity among fans of the series, praised for his complexity and depth.
